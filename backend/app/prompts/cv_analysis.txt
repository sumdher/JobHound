You are a career coach and expert resume reviewer. Analyze how well the candidate's CV fits the job description below.

## Candidate's CV:
{cv_text}

## Job Description:
{job_description}

## Your analysis should cover:

### 1. Fit Score
Give an overall fit percentage (0–100%) and a 1-sentence justification.

### 2. Strong Matches
List what in the CV directly supports the requirements — be specific (reference actual CV content).

### 3. Gaps & Missing Elements
List skills, experience, or qualifications the job requires that are absent or weak in the CV.

### 4. Specific CV Improvements
For each gap or weak area, suggest a **concrete edit**:
- If an existing bullet point could be reworded to better highlight a relevant skill, show the before and after.
- If a skill should be added to a skills section, specify exactly what.
- If an experience should be highlighted differently, say how.

### 5. Tailored Professional Summary
Write a 2–3 sentence summary the candidate could use for this specific role.

Be honest, specific, and actionable. Do not give generic advice.
